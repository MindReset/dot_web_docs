## Book of Answers

Book of Answers gives a quick response when you want a small decision nudge.

-   Includes 350 bilingual Chinese and English random answers.
-   Supports enlarged answer display.

## Controls

1. Long press the bottom button to enter.
2. Short press the bottom button to continue.
3. Short press the top button to go back one step.
4. When an answer appears, short press the bottom button to switch answers.
5. Long press the bottom button to enlarge the answer.
6. Long press the top button to return to the home screen.
