Please follow the steps below to check, or use the Dot. App to inspect the device status:

## Is the content update interval set too long

If you set a long content update interval in the Dot. App, the device will not wake frequently to fetch updates. Please review your settings and shorten the update interval as appropriate.

## Wi‑Fi connection interrupted — check whether your router network is normal

If your device disconnects from Wi‑Fi during use, it will be unable to fetch content updates. Please verify that your router is working properly.
