## Wooden Fish

Wooden Fish is a simple tapping app for clearing small distractions.

-   Tap away worries and add 1 merit point.
-   Records the total number of taps.
-   On firmware 1.2.1 and later, the Wooden Fish counter can be reset from **Settings**.

## Controls

1. Long press the bottom button to enter.
2. Short press the bottom button to add 1 merit point.
3. Long press the top button to return to the home screen.
