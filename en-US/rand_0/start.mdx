## Button Controls

Rand/0 uses two physical buttons:

-   Top button: short press to move up, long press to go back;
-   Bottom button: short press to move down, long press to confirm;
-   Either button: wake the device when it is asleep.

## Accessories and Design

Rand/0 includes both a wide lanyard hole and a standard lanyard hole, so it can be carried with the included lanyard.

The package includes Rand/0 The Pocket Prophet, a custom lanyard, a custom fragrance card, and a user manual.
