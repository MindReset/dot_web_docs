"Holiday" (休息日) is an app that provides holiday information queries with full Siri Intent support. By configuring a shortcut trigger workflow, you can quickly retrieve holiday schedules and sync them to Quote/0.

### Features

- **Holiday Information Query**: Access comprehensive holiday and vacation schedules
- **Siri Intent Support**: Full integration with Siri for voice-activated queries
- **Quote/0 Integration**: Seamlessly sync holiday information to your Dot device
- **Quick Access**: Automated shortcut workflow for instant holiday schedule updates
