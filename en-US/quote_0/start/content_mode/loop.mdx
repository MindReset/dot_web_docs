## Loop Content

Loop Content mode functions similarly to a music player's playlist feature. You can add Telegram Channel, RSS feeds, and photos to the list, and the device will display them sequentially in a continuous loop according to the configured order and interval.

In Loop Content mode, the device will continue rotating through the list until you switch to [Fixed Content](/docs/quote_0/start/content_mode/fixed) mode or manually intervene.

<Callout type="info" title="Mechanisms and Update Intervals">
The mechanism for Loop Content operates as follows:

- The device displays content following the sequence in the loop list;
- In the App's loop list, you can press, hold, and drag to reorder items;
- By tapping "More" next to an item, you can immediately display it on the device;
- Tap "Adjust Interval" above the loop list in the App to modify the global content switching interval.

**Battery Life and Update Frequency:**
When running on battery power, a longer interval extends the device's sleep time between updates, thereby increasing battery life but reducing content freshness. When the device is connected to power, the minimum loop interval can be set to **1 minute**, enabling the highest frequency of content updates.
</Callout>
