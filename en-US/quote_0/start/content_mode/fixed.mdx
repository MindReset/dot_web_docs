## Fixed Content

Fixed Content mode allows users to schedule specific content to be displayed during designated time periods, functioning similarly to a timetable. For example, you can configure the device to display weather information from 7:00 AM to 9:00 AM on weekdays, and Douban Movie rankings from 8:00 PM to 10:00 PM on weekends.

Outside the time periods set for fixed content, the device will automatically switch to [Loop Content](/docs/quote_0/start/content_mode/loop) mode.

<Callout type="info" title="Overlapping Time and Display Logic">
Content can be fixed to specific days of the week or all days within a designated time range. During the display period, content refreshes according to the configured update interval.

When multiple fixed content schedules overlap, the system applies the following priority rules:
1. Prioritize the content with the **earlier end time**;
2. If end times are identical, prioritize the content with the **later start time**;
3. If both start and end times are identical, the device will **randomly select** one of the overlapping contents to display.
</Callout>
