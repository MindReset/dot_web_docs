## How to pair Quote/0

<Callout type="info">
	<include>../../dot/install_app.mdx</include>
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
	Please ensure Quote/0 remains connected to power until pairing is successful.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Connect to power

    Plug the power cable into Quote/0.

    <CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/user_conf_init.png" alt="Quote/0 user activation" />

  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Open the Dot. App

    Make sure the Dot. App is installed on your phone, and register or log in to your account.

    <CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/user_conf_prov.png" alt="Quote/0 waiting for activation" />

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Search for the device

    Open the Dot. App, tap the “+” button at the top right of the home page, and select “Find Device” in the pop-up card. After a moment, your device will appear.

<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-4">
	<CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/search_device_1.png" alt="Search device step 1" />
	<CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/search_device_2.png" alt="Search device step 2" />
	<CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/search_device_3.png" alt="Search device step 3" />
</div>

<Callout type="info">
	Please make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone and the required permission is granted.
</Callout>

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Connect to the network

    Select your Wi-Fi network and enter the correct password, then tap "Send to Device."

    <div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-4">
    <CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/connect_net_1.png" alt="Connect network step 1" />
    <CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/connect_net_2.png" alt="Connect network step 2" />
    <CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/connect_net_3.png" alt="Connect network step 3" />

</div>

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Complete pairing

    After a short wait, your Quote/0 will complete pairing and be added to your device list. When “A Day, A Corner” is displayed, it indicates pairing was successful.

        <div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-4">
    <CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/pair_done_1.png" alt="Complete pairing step 1" />
    <CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/pair_done_2.png" alt="Complete pairing step 2" />

</div>

  </Step>
</Steps>
