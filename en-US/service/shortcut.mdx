<Callout type="info" title="What are Shortcuts?">

Shortcuts on Apple devices let you use apps to perform one or more tasks quickly. The Shortcuts app
lets you create shortcuts with multiple steps. For example, you can build a “Surf Time” shortcut to fetch surf reports, provide an ETA to the beach, and play a surfing playlist.

With the combination of Shortcuts and the Dot. App, you can:

-   Send content to Quote/0 with one tap: create custom shortcuts to quickly push text and images to your device
-   Automate daily tasks: combine Siri, scheduled triggers, and more to keep content fresh automatically
-   Personalized workflows: customize operation flows to match your usage habits

Learn more about Shortcuts at https://support.apple.com/guide/shortcuts/welcome/ios.

    </Callout>
