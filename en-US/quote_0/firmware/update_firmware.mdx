<Callout type="info" title="OTA automatic updates">
	When the device firmware is 2.0.0 or later, the device supports OTA automatic updates. Whether it is powered by USB or battery, it will update automatically at an appropriate time, so you no longer need to use a computer and data cable for updates.

	If your firmware is still earlier than 2.0.0, you still need to complete one final manual firmware update with a computer and data cable.
</Callout>

## Next step

Click “Start Update” and wait until the progress reaches 100%. When “Update Complete” appears, you can disconnect the device.

<Callout type="info" title="Force update">
	If your device is already on the latest version but you still need to perform an update, click "Force Update" to the right of the "Already up to date, disconnect" button.
</Callout>
