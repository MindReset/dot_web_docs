## MBTI Guide

MBTI Guide provides a daily prompt based on your MBTI personality color.

-   Shows daily guidance based on your selected MBTI type.
-   Lets you view the matching personality's guide.

## Controls

1. Long press the bottom button to enter.
2. Short press the bottom button to continue and select a personality type.
3. Short press the top button to switch to the matching MBTI guide.
4. Short press the bottom button to switch to the next guide.
5. Long press the top button to return to the home screen.

## Change Personality Type

Currently, to change the selected personality type, open **Settings**, scroll to **Factory Reset**, and choose again after reset. A future OTA may add personality switching directly inside MBTI Guide.
