## Fortune

Fortune provides a small daily prompt for the current moment.

-   Randomly unlocks 58 lucky colors.
-   Shows daily **do** and **don't** suggestions.
-   Fortune content can only be switched once per day.

## Controls

1. Long press the bottom button to enter.
2. Short press the bottom button to continue.
3. Long press the top button to return to the home screen.
