The Content Studio is an open and active content aggregation platform that spans multiple domains: news, productivity, learning, travel, entertainment, and more. It makes Quote/0 not just a single‑content e‑ink display device, but a small widget with infinite possibilities that integrates into your life—and the Content Studio is the entry point for selecting and editing that content.

## How to use the Shop

The Shop is now available in the Dot. App. On the Dot. App home screen, swipe to the left side, or tap “Shop” at the bottom to enter.
