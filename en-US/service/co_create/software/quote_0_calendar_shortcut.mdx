### Features

- **Calendar Integration**: Seamlessly connects with Apple's native Calendar app
- **Upcoming Events**: Automatically retrieves information about your upcoming scheduled events
- **Quote/0 Display**: Shows calendar events directly on your Dot device's e-ink screen
- **Automated Workflow**: Configure the shortcut to automatically sync calendar information
