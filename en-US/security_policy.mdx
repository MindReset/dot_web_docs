## Overview

We encourage security researchers and users to responsibly disclose security vulnerabilities. By working with us, you help protect our users and systems.

## Reporting Process

<Steps>
<Step>

### Submit a Report

Please email your security vulnerability report to security@mindreset.tech.

</Step>

<Step>

### Acknowledgment

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 24 hours.

</Step>

<Step>

### Assessment and Response

Our security team will assess the report and provide an initial response within 72 hours.

</Step>

<Step>

### Fix and Release

Once the vulnerability is confirmed, we will fix it as quickly as possible and publish a security advisory.

</Step>

</Steps>

## Expectations

-       Please provide as many details as possible, including impact, reproduction steps, and potential remediation suggestions.
-       Do not publicly disclose vulnerability details before a fix has been released.
