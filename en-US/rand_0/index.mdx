Rand/0 The Pocket Prophet is a smart e-ink device you can carry with you.

It is not a serious productivity tool. It is more like a small oracle slip in your pocket. When you get stuck on small questions like “what should I eat today,” “should I go out,” or “should I do this first,” shake it and let the little screen say something first.

The Book of Answers, dice, coin flip, random numbers, and quick decisions can all give you a lightweight result. It does not make the decision for you; it simply helps break the pause and gives you a direction to keep moving.

## What it can do

-   Random decisions: Book of Answers, dice roll, coin flip, random numbers, and quick decisions;
-   Daily companion: personality guide, wooden fish, custom lock screen, and other lightweight content;
-   Daily hints: daily prompts, lucky colors, do/don't suggestions, and daily status;
-   Calendar and time: always-on e-ink display to reduce the need to pick up your phone;
-   Practical tools: Bluetooth presentation remote and NFC card support.

## Use and Care

-   The display uses glass; please protect it from impact and scratches.
-   This product is for entertainment use only. It helps you explore yourself through playful interactions and does not constitute a professional assessment or value judgment.
-   To improve display quality and reduce ghosting, the screen may occasionally perform a full refresh.
-   When cleaning the product, avoid water ingress. Dirt on the product or screen surface can be wiped with a soft cloth moistened with a neutral detergent.
-   Keep the device away from high temperature, high humidity, extreme cold, and other harsh environments.
-   Do not immerse the product in water.
-   Do not replace the battery yourself, as this may cause a fire hazard.
-   When the low-battery prompt appears on the screen, charge it with USB Type-C as soon as possible.
-   Device firmware can be updated through “Wi-Fi Upgrade” in Settings. Please refer to the MindReset official website for the latest details.

<Callout type="warn" title="Notes">

-   E-ink displays are suitable for always-on, low-refresh content, not for frequent animation or rapidly changing visuals.
-   Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC, and shake interactions may be affected by the device system, network environment, and how the device is used.

</Callout>
