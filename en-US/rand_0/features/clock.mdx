## Clock

Clock displays date, time, and weekday on Rand/0.

-   Shows date, time, and weekday.
-   If time has not been calibrated, the device will guide you through Wi-Fi time sync.

## Controls

1. Long press the bottom button to enter.
2. View the current date, time, and weekday.
3. If the device prompts for time sync, follow the prompt to complete Wi-Fi time sync.
4. Long press the top button to return to the home screen.
