## Coin Flip

Coin Flip is a full-scenario random decision tool for quick yes / no or either / or choices.

## Controls

1. Long press the bottom button to enter.
2. Short press the bottom button to start.
3. Shake the device by hand if you prefer physical interaction.
4. Long press the top button to return to the home screen.
