## Next step

Click “Start Update” and wait until the progress reaches 100%. When “Update Complete” appears, you can disconnect the device.

<Callout type="info" title="Force update">
	If your device is already on the latest version but you still need to perform an update, click
	“Force Update” to the right of the “Already up to date, disconnect” button.
</Callout>
