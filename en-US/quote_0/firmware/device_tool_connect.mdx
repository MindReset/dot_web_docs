### After ensuring a stable connection between the device and the computer, click “Connect Device”

<Callout type="info">
	The web tool is compatible with Chromium-based browsers such as Arc, Chrome, Edge, and 360
	Browser. Do not use it on mobile devices or in in-app browsers.
</Callout>

</Step>

<Step>

### Select your device in the pop-up window

<CustomCloudImage osSrc="dot/docs/device_tool_connect_select.png" alt="Select device" />
<Callout type="info" title="If no device is available in the pop-up window">
    Please ensure the device is connected to the computer via a data cable. If there is still no device available, try changing the cable or the computer’s port and try again.
</Callout>
</Step>
