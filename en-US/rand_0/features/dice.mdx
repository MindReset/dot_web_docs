## Dice Roll

Dice Roll supports physical-feeling random rolls on the e-ink screen.

-   Selectable dice quantity: 1, 3, 5, or 9 dice.
-   Real-time physics-style shake animation.
-   Multi-angle dice images.
-   56 x 56 dice graphics in multiple angles.

## Controls

1. Long press the bottom button to enter.
2. Short press the top button to switch dice quantity.
3. Short press the bottom button to start.
4. Shake the device by hand if you prefer physical interaction.
